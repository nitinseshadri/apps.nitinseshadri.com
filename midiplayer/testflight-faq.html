TestFlight Frequently Asked Questions

Thank you for testing out a beta of NS MIDI Player! This page contains answers to some frequently asked questions about TestFlight. If you have any other questions, please contact me or refer to the TestFlight website.

Q: I deleted the beta app, but I am still receiving email notifications from TestFlight. Why?

A: Deleting the app is not enough to stop notifications from TestFlight. If you want to stop receiving notifications, you will need to remove yourself as a tester in the TestFlight app. For more information, please see the "Opting out from testing" section of the TestFlight website.
